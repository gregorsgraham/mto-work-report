How it works

Open the app once, fill in your claim numbers and FMC. Then it runs alongside you in the field.

1

Set up your claims

Enter your title numbers, FMC, and personal details once. The app remembers them across every report you file from then on.

2

Log work in the field

Take Before / During / After photos per site. Mark GPS coordinates. Save your claim boundary before you leave home — then in the back country, see exactly where you are inside your own ground.

3

Stay organized

Costs, sites, photos, and dates all stored by tab in the app. Quick to fill, quick to find when you need them.

What's in the app

📷

Photo capture

Snap Before / (multiple) During / After photos per worksite. Drag-and-drop to re-label if you mix one up. Download all photos as a ZIP to your phone before you leave the site.

🗺️

Offline GPS map

Mark sites with lat/lon or UTM. Save your claim boundaries before you leave home — confirm you're on your own ground out in the back country, even without service.

📅

Good-To Date calculator

The app does the math for you so you don't have to guess your new Good-To date.

💾

Archive past reports

Every report you finish gets archived with its photos. If MTO ever emails asking for a correction, load the old report back up, fix what they flagged, and resubmit.

Install it on your phone

The app runs in your browser — no App Store needed. Add it to your home screen and it opens like a real app, even offline.

Try it out →
On iPhone (Safari):
1. Tap "Try it out" above to open the app
2. Tap the Share icon (square with arrow up) at the bottom of Safari
3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
4. Confirm — the app icon appears like any other app

On Android (Chrome):
1. Tap "Try it out" above to open the app
2. Tap the ⋮ menu (top right)
3. Tap Add to Home screen / Install app

On a laptop: bookmark it or use the install icon in your browser's address bar.
