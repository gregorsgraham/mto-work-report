Paste coordinates from your GPS app
Works with Gaia GPS, onX, Google Maps, or any app that shows coordinates.
Just copy your coordinates and paste them here.
In Gaia GPS: Tap a waypoint → tap the coordinates shown → Copy
In onX: Tap a waypoint or drop a pin → tap the coordinates → Copy
In Google Maps: Hold your finger on the spot → coordinates appear at top → tap to copy. Or paste the full Google Maps link directly.
On a GPS unit: Write down the numbers shown on screen and type them in
All these formats work:
  49.5123, -117.4567  (decimal degrees — most common)
  49°30'44"N 117°27'24"W  (degrees minutes seconds)
  49°30.123'N 117°27.456'W  (degrees decimal minutes)
  N49.5123 W117.4567  (with N/W labels)
  Full Google Maps URL — paste the whole link
